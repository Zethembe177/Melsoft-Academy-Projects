Milk Tart

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together 1/2 cup butter and 1 cup sugar in a medium bowl.
  3. Add 1 egg and beat until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix 2 cups flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Stir flour mixture into sugar mixture until combined.
  6. Press mixture into bottom and sides of two 9-inch pie pans.
  7. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool.
  8. In a large saucepan, combine milk, vanilla, and 1 tbsp butter. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat.
  9. Mix flour, cornstarch, sugar, and eggs in a bowl until smooth.
  10. Slowly whisk into hot milk. Return to heat and boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  11. Pour custard into baked shells. Sprinkle cinnamon on top.
  12. Chill before serving.
